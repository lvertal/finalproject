Course Reflection

WRIT 4662W was the first class I took that addressed the subject of coding itself. As my focus is biology I never learned to code, so this whole class was a learning experience for me.



In the process of learning to create functional digital artifacts, one of my biggest obstacles was aesthetics, or rather my expectations versus the reality of what I was capable of. I quickly found that while many tasks were easy to accomplish, they were much harder to accomplish well.

A major challenge was learning to format my pages in more complex ways.
